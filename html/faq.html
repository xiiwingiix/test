What is the plan to rollout the brand across our physical spaces (Greenlights, GLs, Facilities, Offices)?
This is a phased rollout. We will start with high-impact and easy-to-change brand touch points first and then work our way to those projects with the long lead times.

For now, we will only update branding for new build projects. We do not have a budget to re-brand existing GLs or Physical spaces at this time.

If this is a priority for you and your region, we are happy to work it onto a roadmap. Please contact your regional PM for additional questions.
